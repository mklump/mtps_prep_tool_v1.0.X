Mainframe Modernization: Assessment and Discovery Guide
Chapter 1: About This Guide





A successful mainframe modernization project requires adequate preparation. Lack of preparation leads to lack of information and can result in an incorrect strategy, inaccurate estimates, or an inappropriate architecture. Worse yet, some enterprises incorrectly decide not to undertake development of a modernization strategy because they mistakenly judge that it is too risky and hence fail to gain benefits that competitors achieve.

Purpose of This Guide

The purpose of the Mainframe Modernization: Assessment and Discovery Guide is to explain why you should assess your mainframe environment before you start a modernization project. This guide recommends that a thorough assessment and discovery (A&D) of the mainframe environment is the best way to prepare for a modernization project and explains why such A&D is a necessary prerequisite to a successful project.

This guide presents an overview of the A&D process and the modernization options this process generates. It also provides information about A&D experts who can help you carry out an A&D of your mainframe environment and prepare you to undertake a mainframe modernization project. For details about the benefits of assessing your mainframe environment, see Chapter 2, "Why Undertake Assessment/Discovery?"

About Terminology

In this guide, the term assessment is used in two senses: a general sense and a specific sense. The appropriate meaning for each is generally clear from the context.

  • General sense. Whenever the term assessment is encountered alone, it refers inclusively to the assessment/discovery (A&D) process.

  • Specific sense. The term assessment also refers to the preliminary phase of the two-part assessment/discovery process.

Intended Audience

This guide is intended for the Chief Information Officer (CIO) and other enterprise-level IT decision makers who are responsible for defining the overall technology vision for their enterprise and for aligning this vision with its business strategy. They work closely with the executive management team and decision makers in other departments to identify, recommend, develop, implement, and support cost-effective technology solutions for all aspects of the organization.

Scope

This guide focuses on how your enterprise can become ready to launch a mainframe modernization project. It is an educational and informational guide to the assessment process; it is not a prescriptive guide that prescribes how to plan for and execute an assessment project.

Note:

This guide distinguishes between a process and a project. Process refers to activities aimed at bringing about some outcome—for example, using a questionnaire to obtain a high-level view of the mainframe environment is an activity that is part of the A&D process. Project, on the other hand, refers to the decision by authorized decision makers to commit resources—for example, funds, personnel, equipment, or supplies—to carry out the process within a time frame defined by a start date and an end date.

The guide provides information about A&D services offered by selected, experienced A&D experts you can contact to implement an A&D project. However, it does not describe in detail how these experts plan for and execute their A&D projects—they use their own prescriptive guides for planning and executing their A&D projects.

Summary of Guide Contents

The following shows the organization of the guide and provides a chapter-by-chapter summary of its contents:

  • Chapter 1: About This Guide. This chapter provides an overview of the guide's purpose, content, and organization.

  • Chapter 2: Why Undertake Assessment/Discovery? This chapter explains why an A&D of the mainframe environment is necessary. It discusses challenges created for enterprises by new information technologies and paradigms—for example, the Object-Oriented paradigm, Service-Oriented Architecture, and Open Standards—and how assessment/discovery can be an effective way to prepare the enterprise to meet these challenges.

    The chapter explains that an enterprise must first thoroughly understand its own mainframe environment and the modernization issues it faces. The chapter discusses briefly some major areas of complexity in the mainframe environment that could raise modernization issues. The chapter then provides an overview of the assessment/discovery process and stresses to the reader that modernization specialists may vary in their approaches and terminologies for the assessment/discovery process outlined here.

    The chapter describes the benefits of using assessment/discovery experts. It introduces the six experts whose respective assessment/discovery processes are described in chapters 3 through 8.

    The chapter then describes what next steps enterprises can take after they have read this guide. The chapter ends by providing contact information about these experts and other experts who are members of the Mainframe Migration Alliance.

  • Chapters 3–8: Assessment/Discovery Experts. Each chapter is devoted, respectively, to one of the six selected assessment/discovery vendors. It provides information about the vendor’s services, tools, and methodology that enterprises can use to implement an assessment/discovery project for their mainframe environments. The six experts are: BluePhoenix Solutions, Cognizant Technology Solutions, Fujitsu Consulting, Infosys Technologies Ltd., Micro Focus, and Microsoft Consulting Services.

  • Appendix. The appendix provides sample questions that can be used to formulate a high-level picture of the state of a mainframe environment.

Benefits of Using This Guide

After reading this guide, you will be able to do the following:

  • Conceptualize your mainframe environment and its complexity.

  • Understand why you should assess your mainframe environment before starting a modernization project.

  • Understand what A&D involves and how it can help you identify the most suitable modernization strategy and candidate applications/data for your modernization project.

  • Know about the practical steps you can take to carry out an A&D of your mainframe environment, what experts are available to you to implement an A&D project, and how to access these experts and their resources.
