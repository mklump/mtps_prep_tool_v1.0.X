Mainframe Modernization: Assessment and Discovery Guide
Appendix: Sample Questions for an Assessment/Discovery Project





The following questions about some of the sub-environments form only a small fraction of the total set of questions that a thorough assessment and discovery process would ask. These questions are intended to give you an idea of the types of questions one can ask about each sub-environment and to indicate how such questions can enable you to form a quick, high-level picture of the state of your mainframe environment.

Note:

In addition to sources of information at Microsoft, the following additional sources were consulted for developing these questions: IT Services: Costs, Metrics, Benchmarking, & Marketing, Anthony F. Trdungo, Thomas R. DiPasquale, Robert E. Matthews (Prentice Hall, 2000); IT Production Services, Harris Kern, Rich Schiesser, Mayra Muniz (Prentice Hall, 2004); IT Systems Management: Designing, Implementing, and Managing World-Class Infrastructures, Rich Schiesser (Prentice Hall, 2002); Administering Data Centers: Servers, Storage, and Voice over IP, Kailash Jayaswal (Wiley Publishing, 2006); and Data Center Fundamentals, Mauricio Arregoces, Maurizio Portolani (Cisco Press, 2004).

Questions About the Mainframe Platform (zOS) and the Data Center

Physical Design

  • What flooring design does the data center implement (raised floor, etc.)?

  • What type of power source is used?

  • What type of cooling system is used (HVAC, etc.)? What types of air-conditioning systems do you use?

  • What is the security system to ensure the physical security of the data center?

  • What is the process for cleaning the data center (floor surface, sub-floor, ceiling, equipment, etc.)? What cleaning supplies are used?

  • What is the configuration of power distribution in the data center? Do you use generators? Does the data center experience electrostatic discharge?

  • What is the placement of hardware racks (bottom-to-top, etc.)?

Hardware

Processors

  • What hardware is installed on the computer system under consideration for migration?

  • How are these systems partitioned—for example, multiple mainframes or servers running the Microsoft® Windows® operating system or UNIX operating system?

  • What is the average and peak CPU utilization?

Disk Storage

  • How much DASD is installed on the floor, on the system under consideration, and in the total environment?

  • Are you using SAN type storage? If yes, from which vendors? Is that storage “shared” with your Windows network?

Tape Storage

  • Do you use a Virtual Tape system? If yes, from which vendors? Is that storage “shared” with your Windows network?

Network Connections

  • What is the architecture of your network?

  • What is the procedure for dealing with a situation in which a node goes down?

Operating System

  • On what operating system and OS version does the application run today (OS/390, z/OS 1.4, VSE-ESA, etc.)?

  • What Automated Operations tools/products are in use (BMC MAINVIEW AutoOPERATOR, Unicenter CA-OPS/MVS at, etc.)?

  • What is the approximate elapsed runtime and MIPS consumption?

  • How many transactions per day and MIPS consumption?

  • How many transactions per peak period?

  • How many MIPS are under consideration for migration and how many total MIPS are installed? If your IT Operations are outsourced, what proportion of the total MIPS is outsourced?

  • How is workload transferred?

  • What do you do when a partition fails?

Operations and Systems Programming

  • What performance monitoring tools are currently used (Omegamon, Resolve, etc.)?

  • What resource usage, tracking, or capacity planning tools and repositories are in use?

  • Is cost accounting and chargeback in place? If it is, what product is used (CA JARS, KOMMAND, or the chargeback option to CA Unicenter NeuMICS, etc.)?

  • What is the system’s availability? Is 24 hours a day, seven days a week availability a requirement?

  • How do you monitor your system?

  • How often do you have to apply patches to the operating system?

  • How often does the system experience downtime? What resources do you have to respond to downtime situations?

IBM Software

  • What IBM products are installed on the computer system under consideration for migration? What is the IBM product version for each product listed below?

    • Operating system

    • TP/transaction monitor

    • DBMS

    • Security

Third-Party Software

  • What third-party software products are installed on the computer system under consideration (specify vendor, usage, etc.)?

Production Control

Batch Processing

  • What Job Scheduler is currently used (CA-7, BMC Control/M, Tivoli, etc.)?

  • How do you restart a batch job?

  • Does the automated scheduler control more than one system (multiple mainframes, mid-range systems, Windows systems, or UNIX servers)?

Report and Print Output Management

  • Do you use a print output management system for print directed to paper (IBM AFP or IBM Tivoli Output Manager for z/OS or Barr Systems - BARR/PRINT AFP, BMC CONTROL-D, etc.)?

  • Do you use a report distribution system in lieu of reports printed on paper?

Questions About the Development Environment

Operating System Services

Transaction Processing (TP) Systems

  • For online applications, what TP/transaction monitor is installed (CICS, IMS/TM, etc.)?

  • How many transactions per day? What is their MIPS consumption?

  • How many transactions per peak period?

  • Approximately what availability is required (24 hours a day, seven days a week, etc.)?

File Management and I/O Services

  • What data store do the applications use (VSAM, etc.)?

  • Does the application use Systems Managed Storage?

  • Does the application use tape media? List device types (3420, 3480, silo, etc.)?

  • Are Generation Data Groups used by the applications?

  • What disk devices are used by the application (3390, EMC, etc.)?

Infrastructure

Security and Disaster Recovery Management

  • What security package is used (RACF, CA-Top Secret, CA-ACF2, etc.)?

  • Does the application code interface directly with the System Authorization Facility?

  • Are any special authentication schemes employed by the application (Smartcards, etc.)?

  • Is an interoperable environment with password synchronization and user name mapping required—for example, synchronization between RACF and Windows Security?

  • Are Security Auditing packages used? Describe the auditing procedures.

Resource Management

  • Is a cost accounting and chargeback system in place (home-grown, SAS, etc.)?

  • Is a resource utilization and capacity planning system in place? If so, what packages are used?

  • Is a Service Level tracing and reporting system in place? If so, what packages are used?

Databases and Data Management

  • What databases are currently installed in the environment (IMS, DB2, Adabas, etc.)?

  • What access methods are used (VSAM, BDAM, etc.)?

  • What is the size of each database?

  • For DB2 applications, do you use QMF? Do you use it in batch?

  • How many transactions per peak period?

  • How many transactions per day?

Applications

Applications in General

  • What is the application’s functionality? What does the application do?

  • What is the application type (online, batch, or both)?

  • How is the online application accessed (3270 emulators, MQSeries, other)?

  • For 3270 applications:

    • Have screen-scraping (for example, HLLAPI) programs been built on top of them? Must these applications continue to be used or will they be replaced?

    • If screen-scraping programs were developed, what tools were used to build them (NetManage, Farabi, IBM, other)?

    • Are there other application inter-dependencies? Are those applications also being migrated at the same time? What are these dependencies?

    • Does the application use a Report Management and Distribution System? If it does, which one?

    • Does the application include REXX execs?

    • What other application characteristics should be known (device dependencies like teller terminals, EDI interfaces, file transfers, connection to Real-Time systems, etc.)?

Application Programming

  • What languages are used (vendors and versions)?

  • How many modules are there in total?

  • How many lines of code in total?

  • What source code editor do you use (TSO/ISPF, Natural, etc.)?

  • Does the application contain custom-written assembler programs (sort Exit Routines, etc.)? If it does, what is the extent of their usage (number of modules using them, number of lines using them) and their functionality?

  • Do you currently use interactive program debugging (XPEDITER, etc.)? If you do, which debugger?

  • Do you use the language that came with a third-party database (ADSO for IDMS, IDEAL for Datacom, Natural for Adabas, etc.)?

  • What other languages and development environments do you use for developing and debugging the application on MVS (CoolGen, Easytrieve, IBI, Other 4GL)?

COBOL Applications Development

  • What COBOL compiler and what related tools are used for developing and debugging on MVS (TSO/ISPF, Abend-Aid, other editors, debuggers, or code generators)?

  • Do you currently use Micro Focus Mainframe Express for mainframe development?

  • Do you currently use Micro Focus Net Express COBOL for Windows development? If you do, does it meet your expected future needs? Do you plan to continue its use?

  • Do you currently use Fujitsu NetCOBOL for Windows development? If you do, does it meet your expected future needs? Do you plan to continue its use?

Source Code Management

  • Is the code currently stored in a code management system (Librarian, Panvalet, etc.)?

  • Does the code management system operate on both platforms—mainframe and Windows? If it does not, what do you plan for source code management on Windows?

  • Do you currently have a working method to move your source code from your mainframe to the Windows development platform?

Batch Jobs

  • Approximately how many jobs and programs does the environment contain?

  • What is the approximate elapsed runtime and MIPS consumption?

  • Do the applications use CLISTS or REXX Execs?

  • Do the applications use third-party report writing tools (DYL, Easytrieve)?

Questions About Business Drivers

  • What percentage of your gross revenues go to IT, and how does that compare to your competition? Are you as efficient as your competition?

  • What percentage of your IT budget goes to maintenance rather than new product development? Is that better or worse than your competitors or the industry norm?

  • What percentage of your IT budget goes to the mainframe, and can you justify that this is the best way to allocate those funds?

  • How quickly can IT react to changing business requirements? Can you deliver new products in a shorter time-to-market than your competitors, and is IT an enabler or hindrance in this?

  • Are you developing custom applications to do routine administrative tasks, such as HR or ERP, when commercial off-the-shelf applications might be available? Are there commercial applications available on the Windows platform that you could use?

  • Is IT a strategic resource for you, or a support function? Does your competition have a more effective use of IT?

  • How does your IT infrastructure support new customer acquisition or customer retention, compared to your competition? Are you able to create satisfactory self-service portals or holistic views for your service personnel or customers of the relationship?

  • How is your support organization looking over the next 5–10 years? Do you have training and recruitment plans in place to address your future needs with respect to the existing technologies? How well is the partner ecosystem able to support you: with skilled personnel, services, spare parts, etc?

  • What is your succession plan for your key IT individuals?

  • How well does your IT infrastructure support automated interaction with key partners, such as suppliers, vendors, customers? Are you able to integrate with services provided by them quickly and efficiently?

  • Who is in control of your IT roadmap?

Questions About Quality of Service

Performance

  • What metrics do you use for measuring performance?

Scalability

  • How do you estimate the scalability requirements of your services?

Interoperability

  • What is the extent of interoperability within the total mainframe environment?

  • What metrics do you use to measure the quality of this interoperability?

Reliability

  • What metrics do you use to measure reliability?

  • How do you distinguish between downtime and degraded performance?

  • Are different applications SLA’d at different levels?

  • Do your users sign a Service Level Agreement with IT, and does IT charge back its customers based upon performance?
